# Product Name: InterviewGod.ai

## Core Value Proposition
An AI infrastructure upgrade for talent acquisition teams. It replaces manual, interviewer-dependent hiring processes with consistent, auditable, and bias-reduced AI evaluations at scale. InterviewGod automates the entire flow from initial resume parsing to multi-round technical assessments, operating as a continuous, intelligent screening engine that can speed up hiring by 3.5x.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) & Target Personas
* **Company Profile:** Fast-growing enterprises, post-funding startups, and companies marching toward IPO readiness that process high volumes of candidates.
* **Target Industries:** Technology, Manufacturing, and Logistics & Supply Chain.
* **Economic Buyer:** CEO, Founders, or CHRO.
* **Primary User:** VP of Talent Acquisition, HR Managers, or Technical Hiring Managers.

## Pain Signals to Look For
Look for companies explicitly experiencing or displaying:
* **Capacity Gaps:** Rapid headcount growth mandates without an equivalent increase in HR staff.
* **Operational Drag:** Complaints about slow time-to-hire (e.g., taking weeks to schedule screenings), recruiter burnout, or excessive manual HR administrative tasks.
* **Infrastructure Fragmentation:** Teams struggling with disconnected ATS platforms and job boards, resulting in manual data entry, duplicate statuses, and "lost" candidate data.
* **Compliance/Governance Pressures:** Pre-IPO companies needing standardized, transparent hiring frameworks to prove they are reducing bias and ensuring assessment integrity.

## Key Features & How They Map to Customer Gaps

* **Agentic Assessment & AI Interviews:** * *The Feature:* Conducts real-time conversational interviews and role-specific coding rounds, dynamically evaluating responses and instantly scoring them.
  * *The Gap Solved:* Eliminates the manual screening bottleneck and developer dependency for technical rounds, drastically reducing the burden on internal teams.

* **Audit Trail, Governance & Anti-Cheating:** * *The Feature:* Built-in real-time proctoring utilizing face detection and browser monitoring to deliver 98% cheating detection accuracy.
  * *The Gap Solved:* Solves the need for "IPO-ready" compliance. Removes subjective human bias and provides a defensible, secure audit trail for every hiring decision.

* **Native ATS Integration & Data Sync:** * *The Feature:* Deep, one-click OAuth integrations with platforms like Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, Zoho Recruit, and BambooHR.
  * *The Gap Solved:* Cures "data silos." Stops recruiters from manually transferring candidate statuses between systems and creates a single source of truth for the hiring pipeline.

* **Psychometric & Situational Analysis:** * *The Feature:* Utilizes automatic voice data collection to analyze communication styles and situational responses.
  * *The Gap Solved:* Gives hiring managers deep behavioral and confidence insights without requiring multiple subjective cultural-fit rounds.

* **Multi-Round Hiring Orchestration:**
  * *The Feature:* Automates the progression of candidates through various rounds (e.g., AI Screening to Assessment) based on custom rules and score thresholds.
  * *The Gap Solved:* Eradicates administrative coordination chaos and scheduling conflicts during high-volume scaling phases.
